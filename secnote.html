Secret Notebook Help

Security: Strong AES-GCM 256-bit encryption with PBKDF2 (1,000,000 iterations, SHA-512). Your password is never saved.

Images: Paste images directly or use the 🖼️ button. Images are kept at 100% original uncompressed quality. If files get too large, compress them externally first.

Slideshow: Click 🎞️ to view all inserted images. Use Left/Right arrows to navigate, and Escape to exit.

Fullscreen: Click 🖥️ to enter fullscreen mode. Hover over images and click the ⛶ button to view them in true fullscreen (you can scroll to zoom and click to drag).
